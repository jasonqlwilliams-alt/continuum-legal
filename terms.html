Continuum Orchestrator — Terms & Conditions

Last updated: February 22, 2026

Service Description

Continuum is a personal productivity orchestrator that sends SMS alerts for system events, task reminders, and advisory notifications. This service is operated by Jason Williams for personal use.

SMS Messaging Terms

By opting in to SMS notifications from Continuum, you agree to receive automated transactional text messages related to:

  • System health alerts and critical notifications
  • Task reminders and deadline warnings
  • Advisory messages from the Muse intelligence system
  • Service status updates

Opt-In

SMS notifications are opt-in only. To subscribe to alerts, configure your phone number in the Continuum server environment. By providing your phone number, you consent to receiving automated SMS messages.

Opt-in keywords: Text START or SUBSCRIBE to the Continuum number to confirm your subscription.

Opt-in confirmation message: “You are now subscribed to Continuum alerts. Reply HELP for help, STOP to unsubscribe. Msg&data rates may apply. Max 5 msgs/day.”

Opt-Out

You may opt out of SMS notifications at any time:

  • Reply STOP to any message to immediately unsubscribe
  • Remove your phone number from the server configuration

After opting out, you will receive a final confirmation: “You have been unsubscribed from Continuum alerts. Reply START to resubscribe.”

Help

Reply HELP to any message for assistance. You will receive: “Continuum SMS Alerts — automated notifications from your personal orchestrator. Reply STOP to unsubscribe. For support, contact the account operator.”

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on system activity. Maximum 5 messages per day. Most days you will receive 0–3 messages.

Costs

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan. Continuum does not charge for SMS delivery.

Supported Carriers

SMS delivery is supported on all major US carriers including AT&T, T-Mobile, Verizon, and others. Delivery is not guaranteed and may vary by carrier.

Liability

Continuum is provided as-is for personal use. The operator is not liable for missed or delayed notifications, carrier filtering, or any actions taken or not taken based on SMS alerts.
